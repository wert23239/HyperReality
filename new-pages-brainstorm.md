# Rotting Rights

I'll be honest — I'm truly in a decrepit part of my life.

I get to work at 9am and leave at 7:30. This seems like a flex at some point, but the unfortunate part is that is actually an early night. The only reason we left is because we had two birthdays.

When you hear about burnout you think it's from the hours, but honestly you don't understand the concept. That does not mean shit. Humans really are designed to deliver shareholder values.

Being autistic is all about pattern matching. You follow the fucking trends but you don't really get them. I think people don't understand autism at its purest form.

Radical free will. Using money.
